1. About Streamkar

Streamkar is the #1 social platform for broadcasting and watching live streaming videos. Go live, connect with your favorite stars, and earn rewards and cash!

Starting a Live

1. How do I make a live stream?

You can broadcast on Streamkar from either an iPhone or an Android phone.

• Tap the round camera button in the center at the bottom of the main page.

• Title your broadcast with hashtags so that people with same interests can find you easily.

2. How do I get more viewers in my live stream?

The best way to gain viewers in your broadcast is to:

• Promote your broadcasts by sharing the link on other social networks, such as Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, and Snapchat, and invite your friends to watch before you go live.

3. How do I delete a replay that I created?

To delete a recording, you need to go to your profile page, click “My recording”, tap the recording at the top right corner and select "delete."

4. There are too many comments. Can I remove them?

You can swipe across the screen from the left to the right to clear all information pertaining to comments and gifts.

5. Why is my camera or mic not working?

You must give Streamkar permission to use your camera and/or microphone on your mobile device. Please go to Settings –> Streamkar –> Privacy, and make sure both camera and microphone are enabled.

Coins, Diamonds,Gifts and Cash Out

1. How do I buy Coins?

You may purchase coins by visiting your profile page and tapping on “Coins”. You can also purchase by tapping on the gift box icon in a broadcast and then choosing the “Charge” option.

2. What are Coins and Diamonds?

Coins are the Streamkar currency. Viewers and broadcasters can buy coins and use them to purchase gifts or do coin drops to interact with one another. When viewers send you gifts, you immediately earn the same number of diamonds as the item cost to purchase in the Streamkar Gift Store. Once you have enough diamonds, you can cash them out for real money or convert them back into coins. For a tutorial, please visit: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=e_dmDvFdb3Y

3. I bought coins, but never received them!

If you have purchased coins on Streamkar but they have not showed up in your account, we are happy to help. Please provide us the receipt from either the App Store or Google Play store as well as a screenshot of your profile page for us to track your order quickly.

4. Why have some gift options disappeared from the Gift Shop?

We have many gift ideas, but due to the limited availability of the gift shop slots, we may replace some gifts with new ones from time to time. Please try out the new ones that are equally fun!

7. What is a coin drop/giveaway?

A coin drop is the Treasure Chest gift item, but unlike a normal gift, this allows for coins to be distributed amongst the audience. You can start a coin drop at the cost of 600 coins during anyone's broadcast, including yours. The 600 coins will be won by and randomly distributed to the first ten users in the same broadcast who click the "Snatch" button.

Watching Live?

1. Why can't I view any comments?

If your network is not very stable, you may encounter a broadcast where only your own comments are visible. When that happens, either while you are watching or making a broadcast, you can try exiting the broadcast and re-entering to get it fixed.

Verification and Level

1. How do I get on the Leaderboard of Streamkar?

The leaderboard, which is updated daily, shows broadcasters who have earned the highest number of diamonds within the past 24-hour period.

2. How do Trending People & Tags work?

Trending people/tags is another indicator of a broadcaster's popularity and in turn translates to more exposure.

As users and tags become more popular, they move up the trending list to become more visible to viewers. Broadcasters and hashtags on Streamkar trend with a variety of factors, including but not limited to:

1. Total likes

2. Broadcast activity (comments, likes, and gifts, etc.)

3. Number of viewers

4. How quickly likes accumulate

5. Broadcast length

Social Networks Integration:

Account

1. How do I sign up?

Users can sign up via Facebook, Instagram, a working phone number or Email address (for purposes of authenticating accounts and providing a better social experience.)

2.How to change my personal information?

You can change your username and profile picture directly by clicking them on the profile page.

3. Can I use the Streamkar logo as my profile picture?

Unfortunately, we prohibit use of our official logo, name or likeness as part of a user's account information.

4. I can neither log in nor have I received the verification code.

We suggest that you exit the app and relaunch it. Or you can switch your network connection from Wi-Fi to data. If you are still unable to receive the verification code, you can send the screenshot of your profile page with your phone number to Streamkar@cmcm.com for us to help.

5. How Do I Delete My Account?

Please note that due to security reasons, you must send your registered information along with a screenshot of your profile page to Streamkar@cmcm.com to terminate your account. After it is done, you will no longer be able to log in or view your previous broadcast recordings.

Ban, Block and Suspension

1. How do I unblock someone I blocked by mistake?

To unblock someone you've blocked by mistake, simply navigate to your profile page---Settings---Block list---Unblock then you can remove him from the “Block list”.

2. How do I block someone?

If you notice inappropriate behavior, bullying, or any other rule-breaking conduct on Streamkar, you may block a user from chatting on your broadcast and profile by tapping on their name, navigating to the profile page, and then choosing "block."

3. I've been suspended. What does that mean?

If you are suspended, you are temporarily banned from using most of Streamkar's features as a result of a violation of Streamkar's rules.

4. What is the difference between a ban and a suspension?

The difference between a suspension and ban is that a suspension is temporary and reversible, whereas a ban is final and permanent. Suspension of an account typically lasts between 15 minutes and 48 hours, depending on the severity of the offense.

6. Why am I unable to use the app for my age?

We prohibit children under 13 from broadcasting. If you are over 13 and still cannot use Streamkar, please provide us with your government-issued photo ID as proof of age.

7. I've been banned. What does that mean?

Users are banned for violating Streamkar's rules and policies. Being banned means you are no longer able to log into your account. If you would like to dispute a ban, you may e-mail us at Streamkar@cmcm.com.

Notification

1. I can't receive notifications from this app

Please go to System Settings –> Notification –> Streamkar to make sure the option is enable. Also please make sure the "Do not disrturb" feature is not enabled inside Streamkar.

2. Too many notifications popping up on my phone.

We try hard to make sure all the splendid broadcasts reach you. But you can also choose to disable the notifications in System Settings –> Notification --> Streamkar. Besides, you are able to turn off direct messages if it is too much.
